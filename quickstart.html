Our Story

Established in May 2017, PTE Helper is a public tutoring centre that is located in Melbourne, Australia. PTE Helper offers PTE training courses for Vietnamese students living all around the world. The introduction in 2018 of PTE Helper 50+ plus PTE Helper One-on-one has been an important step in consolidating our centre’s position as a destination of choice for PTE training. The centre now has over eight hundred students from ten nations at various stages of their English levels.
